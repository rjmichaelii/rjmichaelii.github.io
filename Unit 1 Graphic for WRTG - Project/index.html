Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center

Wood Bison Restoration

On the left wood bison are pictured as they are being transported to their new home in Shageluk Alaska. The process started by loading 100 wood bison into special container, then transported to Anchorage, Alaska and finally loaded on a C130 plane and flown to the Innoko valley. Here they are now roaming freely for the first time in 100 years.
