Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center

Wood Bison Restoration

On the left wood bison are pictured as they are being transported to their new home in Shageluk Alaska. The process started by loading 100 wood bison unto special container, transported to Anchorage, Alaska and loaded on a C130 plane and flown to the Innoko valley. Here they are now roaming freely for the first time in 100 years. (This image was taken directly from the AWCC website).

Education

Here is a image captured during one the the Alaska Animals Puppet Shows that are presented to Kindergartners to 2nd graders. In this show students learn about animals in Alaska and are followed by hands-on activities.
