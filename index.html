About Me

My name is RJ, and I am a Gwitch'in Athabascan Alaska Native studying computer science at the University of Alaska Anchorage.

When it comes to topics of computer science that I am interested I am tempted to say many but to name a few, I enjoy artificial intelligence and machine learning. Although, I would love to learn more about software development as well as game and web development. The list continues... but please refer to my CV below for more of my interests. While I have all these interests, I am still discovering my true passion, and when I find time, I love to learn and do projects like this website you are on!

My long-term goals involve contributing my expertise to a tech company or engaging in research to benefit Alaska Natives using computer science. I'm equally committed to fostering a love for computer science among young students in Alaska, hoping to inspire the next generation of innovators.

With a focus on both my academic pursuits and my passion for teaching, I aim to create a brighter future for all. You can view some of the projects that I worked on and my work experience below! If you are interested, feel free to contact me at rjmichaelii@alaska.edu for any comments, questions, or collaboration opportunities!
