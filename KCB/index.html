About Kastyn

I have grown up in Nome, Alaska, my entire life and am currently a soon to be graduate from Stanford University. I will be graduating with my Bachelor's in English with a minor in education.
When I am not studying, you can find me baking up a storm. I am committed to serving up delicious goodies to the community of Nome, and now the Stanford campus! All of my products are carefully handcrafted, baked, and served with a side of love.
